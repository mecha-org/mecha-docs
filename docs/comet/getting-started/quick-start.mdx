# Quick Start

The Mecha Comet is a modular, handheld Linux computer. We are pumped to know that you already have one with you, now lets jump right in.

            :::warning[If your device doesn't turn on]
                It's possible that your device's battery is fully discharged. The recommended power supply for the Mecha Comet is a 5V, 2.5A (12.5W). 
                To turn on your device, connect the Type-C charger to the base Type-C port located at the bottom of the device, press the power (‚èª) button, you will see a green LED turn on and the display will show the Mecha logo.
