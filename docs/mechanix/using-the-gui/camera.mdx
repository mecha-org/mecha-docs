The Mecha Comet features a 5-megapixel camera with autofocus, making it ideal for multimedia applications such as photography and computer vision tasks. The camera is complemented by dual digital microphones for high-quality audio capture.  

The Camera App in Mecha Comet is a simple application photography, leveraging the device's 5 MP auto-focus camera. 

### Accessing the Camera App  

The Camera App comes pre-installed on the Mecha Comet. You can launch it using:  

- **App Drawer** – Swipe up on homescreen, tap on Camera App to open.  
- **Terminal Command** – Run the following command:  

  ```sh
  $ mechanix-camera
  ```  

### Adjustable Resolution  

The Camera App allows users to adjust the resolution for capturing images,

This flexibility lets users balance quality and storage needs based on their preferences.  


### Storage Location  

Images captured using the Camera App are stored in the device’s internal storage at:  

```sh
~/Documents/Pictures/
```
